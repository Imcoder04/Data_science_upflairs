Insurance Premium Charges Prediction

Welcome to our project...

The "Insurance Premium Charges Prediction" project aims to accurately predict the minimum insurance premium charges for individuals based on a set of personal and health-related factors. This project leverages statistical and machine learning techniques to provide users with an estimated premium cost, helping them to better understand and manage their potential insurance expenses.

Project Details

Key Features

  • User Information Input:
    • Age: The age of the individual, which significantly impacts insurance costs due to risk factors associated with different age groups.
    • Sex: The gender of the individual, as insurance premiums can vary between males and females.
    • BMI (Body Mass Index): A measure of body fat based on height and weight, influencing health-related risks and, consequently, insurance costs.
    • Children: The number of children the individual has, as dependents can affect insurance premiums.
    • Smoker Status: Indicates whether the individual smokes, a critical factor since smokers often face higher insurance charges due to increased health risks.
    • Health Category: A classification of the individual’s overall health status, which can impact premium calculations.
    • Region: The geographical location of the individual, as insurance rates can vary based on regional healthcare costs and regulations.
  • Prediction Model:

    • The project utilizes advanced machine learning algorithms to analyze the input data and predict the minimum insurance premium charges. The model is trained on a dataset containing similar user profiles and their corresponding insurance costs.

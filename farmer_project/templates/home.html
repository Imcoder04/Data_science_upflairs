Farmer's Crop Prediction System

Overview

The "AI Farmer Guide" is designed to assist farmers in selecting the optimal crop based on various factors such as temperature, pH levels, and the amounts of nitrogen, oxygen, and potassium in the soil. This tool aims to enhance crop production by providing tailored recommendations that align with the specific conditions of their farmland.

Project Details

Dependent Factors:-

  • Temperature: The average temperature of the farming area, which influences the growth and health of crops.
  • Humidity: The moisture level in the air, essential for crop transpiration and growth.
  • pH Level: The acidity or alkalinity of the soil, affecting nutrient availability and microbial activity.
  • Nitrogen: A key nutrient that supports plant growth and leaf development.
  • Phosphorus: Essential for root development and energy transfer within the plant.
  • Potassium: Important for water regulation, enzyme activation, and overall plant health.
  • Rainfall: The amount of precipitation, crucial for water supply to crops.

Technologies and tools:-

    Python Libraries:-

  • Flask: Used for developing the web application and creating a user-friendly interface.
  • NumPy: Utilized for numerical computations and handling arrays.
  • pandas: Employed for data manipulation and analysis.
  • scikit-learn: Used for implementing machine learning algorithms and data preprocessing.

    • Machine learning Algorithm:-

  • K-means Clustering: Applied for grouping similar data points and identifying patterns in the data.
  • StandardScaler: Used for normalizing data to ensure that each feature contributes equally to the analysis.
